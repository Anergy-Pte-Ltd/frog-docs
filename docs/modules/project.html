Project Module¶

Revision Details

Author: Matthew Martella Updated: 23/11/2021

Objective¶

Modify and enhance the original project module from the community and enterprise editions to:

  • Cover all types of work by Anergy

  • Provide an effective interface to the work as required

Proposed Model¶

Basic Model¶

project (module level)

At the module/model level shall mean the collection and hierarchy of work objects as defined by this list of definitions

work

Work is the past, present and future discharge of all tasks, costs and potential obligations within the business. Work is arranged in a tree hierarchy such that: –parent elements are completely defined by all child elements –only childless elements can have tasks or costs attached –all elements have unique identifiers (WID) for their level –the identifiers of all elements belong to a specific scheme which can add additional meta-data
